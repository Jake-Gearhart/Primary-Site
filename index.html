About

Jake Gearhart is a Chicago-based composer specializing in writing music for film, games, and other media. He is currently attending the MFA Composition for the Screen program at Columbia College in Chicago, and received a Bachelors Degree in Music Composition with a minor in Computer Science from the same institution.
